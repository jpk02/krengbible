Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 10, 2026

krengbible ("the app," "we," "us") is a bilingual Korean/English Bible reading app. This page explains what information the app collects, how it's used, and how to reach us.

What we collect

If you create an account (email and password), we collect:

  • Email address — used only to sign you in and sync your data across devices.
  • Your content — highlights, saved verses, reading progress, and Quiet Time (QT) journal notes you create in the app.

This content is stored in our database (Firebase/Firestore, operated by Google) under your account, and is not visible to other users or shared with anyone.

If you don't create an account, the app still stores your reading progress, highlights, and settings — but only locally on your device, never sent anywhere.

Usage analytics

The app uses Firebase Analytics (Google) to understand how the app is used — which screens are viewed and which features are used — so we can improve it. This is first-party analytics about app usage only: it is not used to track you across other companies' apps or websites, and no advertising identifier is collected. Analytics data is not linked to your name or email address.

What we don't do:
  • No advertising, no ad networks
  • No cross-app or cross-site tracking
  • No selling or sharing your data with third parties
  • No access to your contacts, photos, camera, or location

Third-party services

The app relies on a small number of services to function:

  • Firebase (Google) — account sign-in, storage for your synced content, and app usage analytics (see above).
  • Cloudflare Workers — our backend API that serves Bible text and generates devotional content.
  • Bible text providers (대한성서공회/Korean Bible Society, Crossway/ESV, Tyndale House/NLT, Biblica/NIV, NavPress/The Message) — these provide the Scripture text itself; no personal data is shared with them. See the app's Attribution & Copyright screen for full licensing details.
  • Unsplash — supplies the background photo shown on the Verse of the Day card.

AI-generated content

Some devotional reflections and section-heading translations in the app are generated using Anthropic's Claude API from Bible text alone — no personal or account information is sent to this service.

Data retention and deletion

Your synced data is kept as long as your account exists. You can permanently delete your account and its synced data at any time from within the app: Settings → Delete Account. If you'd rather we do it for you, email the address below.

Children's privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date above will change accordingly.

Contact

Questions, feedback, or a data deletion request — reach us at inbox@krengbible.com.
