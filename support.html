Common questions

How do highlights, notes, and reading progress sync across devices?
Sign in from Settings with an email account. Once signed in, your highlights, notes, folders, reading progress, and QT journal entries sync automatically.

Can I read without an internet connection?
Yes. From Settings, you can download individual books or the entire Bible for offline reading.

What translations are available?
Korean: 개역개정 (NKRV). English: ESV, NLT, NIV, and MSG. You can switch translations from the Reader.

How do I delete my account?
From Settings → Account, choose Delete Account. This permanently removes your account and all synced data.

Something looks wrong with a verse, translation, or footnote.
Email us the reference (book, chapter, verse) and what looks off — we check every report against the source text.
